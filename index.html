Editor Guide & Markdown Cheatsheet

Text-to-Speech (TTS)

Syntax Cleaning

Markdown symbols are ignored during speech to keep it natural, but we still track your position in the code exactly.

Tracking

Highlight text or place your cursor. Press Play to start from that point. The editor highlights words as they are spoken.
